SIMPLE, ROBUST AND ADVANCED TO ITS ROOTS

Our twitter based model is a hybrid model having characteristics of both extractive summarisation and abstractive summarisation. This gives it the power to find information as efficiently as possible whilst also providing a human touch to the tweets generated by virtue of Natural Language Processing tools used in the Neural Network based Abstractive model.

MODULAR AND SUPPORTED BY MANY WEB APPS

Modules used for both of the products are modular and are made taking care of the requirement of different Web applications and their respective backends. Both of the type of products are also made of exclusive modularly coded summarisation modules which happen to be broken down and used elsewhere.

ADAPTIVE NATURE OF NEURAL NETWORKS

Our model is exclusively based on Neural Networks and thus takes use of its adabtability to different datasets without changing the internal code of the system. The only thing user needs to do is to train the model on an entirely different datasets and observe the rapid adaptation of how a twitter based module can also work on news headlines and their corresponding summary

VIGOUROUSLY TRAINED TO LEAVE NO SPACE FOR ERROR

The more you train the more you achieve. We have implemented this technique with our networks too. Trained rigourously for 8 hours daily for almost a week, they have strengthened themselves to the ups and downs of the wild data out there. Our dataset is also available online.
